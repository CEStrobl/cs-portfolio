The Benefits of Copyrights in Programming

CSCI 301 - Grade: 100

This essay discusses how programmers can protect and redistribute their code using different copyright options, such as traditional copyrights, copyleft, permissive licenses, and proprietary licenses. It highlights the importance of choosing the right license based on the intended use and adhering to ethical standards when using outsourced code. The essay concludes with a reminder for programmers to act responsibly with their own code and respect the rights of others.

Ethical Practices in Cybersecurity

CSCI 325 - Grade: 100

This essay highlights the importance of ethics in cybersecurity, focusing on privacy, harm prevention, and adherence to laws and policies.
