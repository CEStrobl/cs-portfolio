Python Right Triangle Calculator

CSCI 301 - Grade: 100

This Python project defines a RightTriangle class that calculates essential properties of right triangles. It includes getter and setter methods for the two legs and provides methods to compute the hypotenuse and surface area. For example, with sides 3 and 4, the program outputs a hypotenuse of 5.0 and a surface area of 6.0, demonstrating its utility for geometric calculations.
